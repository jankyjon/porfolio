About

This long scrolling page demonstrates my progression in art through the years starting with 2014.
I wanted to make this a fun simple experience for users when they view my art.
I started this assignment with the idea of using my artwork, and thought a timeline would be fun.
I first started with the gradient and then created a long line png image with photoshop.
Then I placed my artworks accordingly to the shape of the whimsical line.
I used Jqueary to get the website to respond and move pieces of information around.
